Nomination Process Begins as Tamil Nadu Moves Into Formal Poll Phase

Compiled by Election Desk - March 30, 2026

Tamil Nadu has entered the nomination phase for the 2026 Assembly election. According to district election notifications reflecting the official schedule, nomination filing begins on March 30 and closes on April 6.

The next milestones are scrutiny of nomination papers on April 7 and the final withdrawal deadline on April 9. Polling is scheduled for April 23, and counting is scheduled for May 4.

Election timeline snapshot: Polling on April 23, 2026, counting on May 4, 2026.

Campaign activity has intensified in parallel. Recent Tamil Nadu election reports tracked high-profile rallies, alliance negotiations, and continued focus on welfare-oriented promises aimed at women, youth, and household budgets.

Coalition positioning remains central to the race. Reports in early March noted seat-sharing negotiations among key alliances and district-level campaign mobilization as parties prepared for nomination filing week.

Window: March 30 to April 6 (Nominations) Category: Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 Next checkpoint: Scrutiny on April 7
